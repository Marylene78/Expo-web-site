About us

Welcome to our tufting business! We specialize in creating beautiful and unique tufted decor items.

Eco Tufts and Interiors is a company dedicated to crafting sustainable and stylish home essentials. We specialize in hand-tufted mats that elevate your living spaces while minimizing our environmental impact. Our skilled artisans meticulously create door mats, bathroom mats, and seating room mats, using responsibly sourced materials. We believe in the power of design to enhance comfort and beauty, and our products reflect this commitment.
  • Our product range includes:
    • Door Mats: Welcoming guests with style and functionality.
    • Bathroom Mats: Adding a touch of luxury to your bathroom.
    • Seating Room Mats: Enhancing comfort and aesthetics in your living space.
    By choosing Eco Tufts and Interiors, you're not just selecting a product; you're making a conscious choice to support sustainable practices and enhance your home with timeless elegance.
